Our Mission & Values

At Paws & Hope, we believe that compassion knows no species. Founded in 2015, our organization is dedicated to the welfare of stray and abandoned animals throughout South Africa.

We operate on the core values of empathy, integrity, and action. Our team of dedicated volunteers and veterinarians work tirelessly to provide medical care, shelter, and love to those who cannot speak for themselves.

Through education and community outreach, we strive to create a sustainable future where animal welfare is a priority for everyone in our beautiful country.
